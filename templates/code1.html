 CODE OF PARTICIPANTS



    

  1.  Participants are required to refrain from harassing behavior towards organizers and other participants, sponsors, media personnel, volunteers, and  staff Harassment includes

      
       
    1.   Offensive  verbal comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race and religion.
       
    2.   Sexual images in public spaces deliberate intimidation, stalking, photography recording without explicit consent, sustained disruption of talks or other events.
        
    3.  Inappropriate physical contact, unwelcome sexual attention.
        
    
  
  2.  Attendees asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately.
  
  3.  Participants must take care of their belongings and components as organizers are not responsible for any of their things.
   
  4.  Smoking and consumption of alcohol is strictly prohibited during the course of the fest.
   
  5.  ID card of your respective college is compulsory.
   
  6.  The judges and organizers words are the final judgment and no sort of arguments will be entertained.
   
  7.  If an attendee engages in behavior that violates this code of conduct, the fest organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender or expelling them from the premises with no refund.
   
  8.  We hope that you will help make IGNUS-19 a welcoming, friendly event for all.
